RiceBot V3.0

RiceBot is a bot for learning and mining rice on the site FreeRice.

How To Use

Drag this RiceBot link to your favorites bar. Navigate to FreeRice. Click RiceBot in your favorites. Voila! The Mining has begun.

Databases

Additionally you can add prebuilt databases to RiceBot. Simply drag the database you want from the links below link to your favorites bar. Navigate to FreeRice. Click the database to import. Then click RiceBot in your favorites.
